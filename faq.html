FAQ

  • What types of companies do you work with?
    The list of industries and types of companies we have experience with is too vast to name them all, however, the following type of consulting services provided by our firm:

    • Engineering services
    • Construction
    • Specialty equipment manufacturing
    • Training
    • Technology firms (research & development)
    • Transmission and Distribution companies
    • Website & digital marketing
    • Etc.
  • What are your consulting rates?
    We provide our clients with a not-to-exceed cost estimate before beginning any work. No one likes surprises, so we work with our clients to understand their specific business needs and then outline our plan of action and the associated costs to implement those solutions.
  • Why would my company need a consulting firm?
    Typically, our clients are experiencing one or more of the following scenarios: • Struggling with challenges or obstacles within their business that is holding their company back from achieving growth and higher profitability.
    • Challenged by scaling their business or developing and implementing a growth plan that actually delivers intended results.
    • The company has outgrown its existing business processes, causing problems with daily operations (errors, increased costs, unreliable results, slower than expected output).
  • Can you complete the work before the planned schedule?
    Yes. We will try to complete the work prior to the schedule with additional resources, where applicable/possible. We typically work on multiple projects at any point in time, and rush projects can strain our schedules.
  • What is your refund policy?
    Please refer to our refund policy in detail.
  • What can I expect from the initial consultation?
    Our firm is built on strong values and the belief that ‘our success is achieved by helping you be successful. The initial consultation is to provide full transparency about who we are as a company and how we might help your business.
  • Do you work with start-ups?
    If you’re an entrepreneur developing or refining your business model, identifying target markets, defining your brand, mapping your business operations, strategizing your company launch, or seeking expertise in launching your company, then our firm can bring significant impact and expertise to your start-up.
  • How does the process work?
    Every situation is unique. However, some fairly common steps include the following:

    • Initial discussion to understand your basic needs.
    • Review your existing materials (draft business plans, financials, market research, competitor research, etc.).
    • Follow-up discussion to clarify details and agree upon the scope and fees for the project. • Several in-depth discussions to better understand the details, develop strategies, and agree on all major elements of the business plan and financial model.
    • Conduct additional research, as needed.
    • Draft an Investor Presentation and review it with you to ensure that all parties agree on the high-level vision.
    • Draft the Executive Summary and review it with you to ensure that we all agree on the next level of detail.
    • Prepare a draft outline of the business plan for your review.
    • Begin filling in the details on the draft. Obtain and incorporate your feedback. • Incorporate your revenue and expense models into the financial model, and explain how it works so that you can take “ownership” of the financials.
    • Finalize all deliverables.
    • Coach you on the best way to deliver the investor presentation and on how to address questions.
    • Provide ongoing support, including making minor updates to the deliverables at no additional charge.
  • What are the benefits engaging a Consultant Firm for the Project?
    There are many benefits to engaging a consultant firm for a project. Here are some of the most but not limited to the following:

    Expertise: We have specialized & expert knowledge and experience in a wide range of areas, right from the conceptual design, detailed engineering & deliverables, supply chain management, project management until successful completion, handing over & close out of the project. This will help you to ensure that your project is completed on time and within budget.

    Objectivity: We can give a more objective perspective to your project which will be helpful in identifying and addressing problems and in making sure that your project is aligned with your overall business goals.
    Time Savings: We can help you to save time by taking on tasks that you may not have the expertise or resources to do yourself. This can free up your time so that you can focus on other aspects of your business.
    Cost Savings: We can help you to save money by identifying cost saving opportunities and by negotiating better deals with vendors which will help you to avoid costly mistakes.

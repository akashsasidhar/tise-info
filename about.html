HSE POLICY

HEALTH, SAFETY AND ENVIRONMENT

TISE Consultant is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for all employees, contractors, and visitors. We are also dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact through responsible practices. This policy outlines our core principles and expectations for achieving these goals.
• Complying with all relevant health, safety, and environmental (HSE) legislation and regulations.

• Providing adequate resources to ensure the safe conduct of all work activities.

• Fostering a culture of safety where all employees are empowered to identify and report hazards.

• Minimizing our environmental footprint through responsible use of resources, waste reduction, and pollution prevention.

• Expedient action to correct or isolate unsafe conditions or work practices.

• Employee accountability for role-related HSE requirements.

• Reasonable effort to assure that employees can accomplish assigned tasks safely and with no harm to the environment.

• Activities to enhance workers’ awareness and participation of HSE requirements.

• Ongoing monitoring and improvement of the HSE management system.

• To stay relevant, the policy is periodically reviewed by the senior management.

TISE Consultant management is fully committed to the requirements and the spirit of this HSE plan and to continual improvement of our HSE performance.

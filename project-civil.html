Multistorey Residential Tower

Project Details
  • Name:Multistorey Residential Tower
  • Duration:36 months
  • Client:Marilin De Aragon

Project Description

Our consultancy services start from site investigation and geotechnical analysis, feasibility study and planning, site evaluation and due diligence, concept design and development, tender preliminary design, detail design, pre-bid, and post-bid tender evaluation, support to contract awards with clients, project design review after contracts award, and site supervision during the construction, commissioning of building systems until issuance of Preliminary and Final Acceptance Certificates.

Our responsibility is to provide consultancy services for the construction of new Multistorey Residential Tower including but not limited to the following:
Our services cover the major work activities of preliminary and detailed design preparation, 3D models, pre-bid and post-bid support until contract award, project management, site supervision during the construction, risk management and mitigation strategies, quality control checks throughout the construction process, implementing monitoring environmental, health & safety standards and handing over of the new residential tower and warranty supervision until issuance of the Final Acceptance Certificate.

What is Challenge for client?

While EPC offers a streamlined approach, clients face some unique challenges:

• Limited Control: Client relinquishes significant control over design and procurement decisions to the EPC Contractor.
• Risk Allocation: Carefully review the EPC contract to understand risk allocation for cost overruns, delays, or unforeseen issues.
• Performance Guarantees: Negotiate clear performance guarantees from the EPC Contractor regarding quality, schedule, and budget.
• Communication and Coordination: Maintain open communication with the EPC Contractor and Consultant to ensure project alignment.
• Limited Contractor Pool: Selecting a qualified EPC Contractor with experience in multi-storey residential projects is crucial.

Providing Solutions From Start to The end

Throughout the EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) project lifecycle for constructing multistorey residential tower building, the consultant will provide a comprehensive range of solutions to ensure a smooth and successful project. Here's a breakdown of the consultant's support:

  • Feasibility Study and Site Analysis: The consultant will assess the project's viability, considering factors like zoning regulations, soil conditions, and market demand. They'll also analyze the site for potential constraints and opportunities.
  • Concept Design and Architectural Planning: The consultant will develop initial design ideas that meet your program requirements, budget, and aesthetic preferences. This may include space allocation, building form, and architectural style.
  • Detailed Engineering Design: The consultant will develop detailed construction drawings and specifications that comply with building codes and standards. This includes structural, mechanical, electrical, and plumbing (MEP) engineering designs.
  • Permitting and Approvals: The consultant will guide you through the permit application process, liaising with relevant authorities to obtain necessary approvals for construction.
  • Operations and Maintenance Support: The consultant may provide ongoing support with building operations and maintenance manuals and recommendations for optimal building performance.
